GSAP is a framework-agnostic JavaScript animation library that turns developers into animation superheroes

Performance :-

GSAP is known for its exceptional performance. It leverages hardware-accelerated CSS animations and SVG, ensuring smooth and efficient animations even on devices with limited resources.

Cross-browser compatibility :-

GSAP works consistently across all major browsers, including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, and Internet Explorer, providing a consistent experience for users..

Rich features :-

GSAP offers a wide range of features and functionalities for animation, including tweening (smoothly transitioning between CSS properties), timeline control (sequencing and synchronization of animations), and plugins for handling complex animations like motion paths, physics-based animations, and more.

Ease of use :-

Its syntax is straightforward and easy to understand, making it accessible for both beginners and experienced developers. GSAP uses a chaining syntax, allowing you to create complex animations with simple, readable code.
